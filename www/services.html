Habermann Aurum Pumpen Gmbh
since 1927

Habermann Aurum Pumpen is one of the leading manufacturers of centrifugal pumps for handling solids-laden liquids. Our credo is to meet the demanding requirements of the market and to support our customers on site in the best possible way.

NKMZ
since 1934

“Novokramatorsky mashinostroitelny zavod” is the largest heavy engineering enterprise of tailor-made production in Europe. The company has been successfully operating for 85 years in the market of metallurgical, mining, press-forging, sinking and tunneling, handling and specialized equipment. The rich history of the company includes a lot of achievements marked as “created for the first time ever”, including items for the space industry. NKMZ equipment is operated in 79 countries, including France, Italy, Germany, and Japan. The quality management system of the enterprise complies with the requirements of the international standard EN ISO 9001:2015, which is confirmed by the certificate issued by the international certification body TUV Thuringen (Germany). We offer the most complete and comprehensive solutions for design, supply, commissioning and maintenance of equipment throughout its service life. NKMZ is always open for cooperation.

RAKURS LLC

The company has more than 60 employees. There is a scientific and design parts, equipment department.

The scientific part includes laboratories and a pilot factory for testing crushing, processing, flotation and pelletizing of ferrous metal ores. The scientific part consists of two candidates of sciences, graduate students and leading specialists with extensive experience in the production facilities of the mining industry and metallurgy.

The design part includes the bureau of Chief Project Engineers, the technological, construction, and plumbing departments, as well as the department of electrical engineering and automation of technological processes. The main composition of the departments is the leading specialists of "SRDI "Mekhanobrchormet" PJSC, who have an average experience in the design of industrial facilities of 15-20 years, many of them more than forty years.

"SPA "RAKURS" LLC carries out pre-design studies, technical and economic calculations, feasibility studies, project - Engineering, Basic Engineering (BE), Detail Engineering (DE), Final Documents (FD). The company also performs industrial 3D modeling.

TITANIUM INSTITUTE
since 1954

JOINT STOCK COMPANY “INSTITUTE OF TITANIUM” (JSC “Institute of Titanium”) is the only comprehensive research and design organization in Europe in the field of titanium and magnesium production, primary nonferrous metallurgy.

JSC “Institute of Titanium” combines the design part (TITANProekt), the research part (NIITitan) with the Center for certification tests, standard samples and metrological support of analytical control (TsSIMAK), the pilot plant (ZMOZ). Such a structure provides an integrated approach to the execution of work – from the development of new technical solutions and design and engineering work to the introduction of highly efficient technologies and the achievement of warranty indicators.

JSC «Titanium Institute» has a certified quality management system in the field of design and engineering work in accordance with the requirements of the ISO 9001: 2015 standard.
